Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 27, 2026

Overview

ZerverMonitor ("the App") is a server monitoring dashboard for iOS and watchOS. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App does and does not collect.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect personal information of any kind
  • We do not require account creation or login
  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or contact information
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not use analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks
  • We do not use cookies

Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • App preferences: Display settings stored via standard iOS mechanisms (UserDefaults).

This data is not transmitted to us or any third party. You can delete it at any time by removing the App from your device.

Network Requests

The App makes network requests to a monitoring backend server to fetch server status data. These requests:

  • Do not include any personal or device-identifying information
  • Contain only standard HTTP headers
  • Are used solely to retrieve server metric data (uptime, memory, request counts, etc.)

The backend URL is configured at build time. The App does not communicate with any other servers.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics services, advertising networks, or social media frameworks.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we will post the revised version on this page with an updated date. Material changes will be communicated through an App update.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at our Support page.
